Jersey Flights Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Jersey Flights is an Alexa skill designed to provide live flight information for Jersey Airport.

Information collected

The skill stores only the information necessary to provide its features.

This may include:

* flight tracking preferences
* named passengers that you choose to track
* your personalised journey time to Jersey Airport
* your preferred arrival hall timings for hand luggage and hold luggage

These settings are stored securely within Amazon Web Services (AWS) and are associated only with your Alexa account.

Information not collected

Jersey Flights does not collect or store:

* your name
* your address
* payment information
* precise location
* contacts
* microphone recordings
* personal documents

Third-party services

The skill retrieves publicly available flight information from Jersey Airport and may use publicly available aviation information to provide live flight updates.

Data sharing

No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

Data retention

Your settings remain stored until you delete them or disable the skill.
